# Digital Lending Platform - Financial Projections

## Revenue Model

### Loan Products
1. **Personal Loans**: $5,000-$50,000, 3-7 year terms, APR 7.99%-24.99%
2. **Small Business Loans**: $10,000-$500,000, 1-5 year terms, APR 9.99%-29.99%
3. **Auto Refinancing**: $10,000-$75,000, 2-6 year terms, APR 5.99%-18.99%

### Revenue Streams
- Interest income: 85% of total revenue
- Origination fees: 12% of total revenue (1-5% of loan principal)
- Late fees and servicing: 3% of total revenue

## Financial Projections (Year 1-3)

**Year 1:**
- Loan originations: $1.2 billion
- Loans funded: 25,000
- Average loan size: $48,000
- Revenue: $168 million
- Operating expenses: $142 million
- EBITDA: $26 million (15.5% margin)

**Year 2:**
- Loan originations: $3.4 billion
- Loans funded: 68,000
- Average loan size: $50,000
- Revenue: $476 million
- Operating expenses: $343 million
- EBITDA: $133 million (28% margin)

**Year 3:**
- Loan originations: $5.8 billion
- Loans funded: 112,000
- Average loan size: $51,800
- Revenue: $812 million
- Operating expenses: $520 million
- EBITDA: $292 million (36% margin)

## Capital Requirements

- Technology development: $18 million

**Growth Capital (Year 2):**

- Marketing expansion: $25 million

## Unit Economics

Average cost per funded loan: $1,200
- Customer acquisition: $420
- Underwriting and processing: $280
- Servicing (annual): $180
- Credit losses (annual): $320

Average revenue per funded loan (lifetime): $6,400
- Interest income: $5,440
- Origination fee: $960

Lifetime value (LTV): $6,400
Customer acquisition cost (CAC): $420
LTV:CAC ratio: 15.2:1 (healthy benchmark is >3:1)

## Break-Even Analysis

Monthly fixed costs: $11.8 million
Contribution margin per loan: 73%
Break-even: 2,150 loans per month ($103 million monthly originations)
Expected timeline to break-even: Month 18
