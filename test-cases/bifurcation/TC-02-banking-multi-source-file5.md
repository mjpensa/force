# Digital Lending Platform - Go-to-Market Strategy

## Target Customer Segments

### Primary Segment: Millennial Professionals (Ages 28-40)
- Income: $65,000-$150,000 annually
- FICO score: 680-750
- Use case: Debt consolidation, home improvement, major purchases
- Size: 47 million potential borrowers in US
- Acquisition channel: Digital marketing (Google, Facebook, Instagram)
- Expected conversion rate: 8.2% of applicants

### Secondary Segment: Small Business Owners
- Business revenue: $250,000-$5 million annually
- Personal FICO: 700+
- Use case: Working capital, equipment financing, expansion
- Size: 12 million small businesses in US
- Acquisition channel: Partnerships with accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero)
- Expected conversion rate: 12.4% of applicants

### Tertiary Segment: Auto Refinance
- Vehicle value: $15,000-$60,000
- Current loan APR: >8%
- Refinance savings potential: >$1,500
- Size: 23 million auto loans eligible for refinancing
- Acquisition channel: Comparison sites (LendingTree, Bankrate)
- Expected conversion rate: 6.1% of applicants

## Marketing Strategy

### Year 1 Budget Allocation: $12 Million
- Digital advertising: $5.4 million (45%)
  * Paid search: $2.1 million
  * Social media: $1.8 million
  * Display/programmatic: $1.5 million
- Partnership marketing: $3.6 million (30%)
  * Affiliate network: $2.2 million
  * Co-marketing with brands: $1.4 million
- Content marketing and SEO: $1.8 million (15%)
- Traditional/brand marketing: $1.2 million (10%)

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Targets
- Digital channels: $380-$450 per funded loan
- Partnership channels: $520-$680 per funded loan
- Organic/referral: $120-$200 per funded loan
- Blended CAC target: $420 per funded loan

## Competitive Positioning

**Value Proposition:**
"Get approved in 10 minutes, funded in 24 hours, with rates 3% lower than traditional banks"

**Differentiation:**
- Speed: 10-minute approvals (vs. 3-7 days for traditional banks)
- Technology: Mobile-first experience, 4.7-star app rating target
- Transparency: No hidden fees, clear rate disclosure
- Flexibility: No prepayment penalties, flexible payment dates

**Competitive Analysis:**
- **vs. SoFi**: Lower rates for prime borrowers, better customer service (live chat in <2 min)
- **vs. LendingClub**: Faster approvals, lower fees (1% origination vs. 2-5%)
- **vs. Upstart**: More loan products (personal, business, auto), larger loan sizes
- **vs. Traditional banks**: 10x faster, 100% digital, competitive rates

## Launch Timeline

**Q1 2026: Pre-Launch**
- Brand development and website launch
- Partnership agreements (2-3 major partners)
- Early access waitlist (target: 50,000 sign-ups)

**Q2 2026: Soft Launch**
- Limited beta (5 states: CA, TX, FL, NY, IL)
- 500 loans funded
- Test and optimize conversion funnel

**Q3 2026: Regional Expansion**
- Expand to 20 states
- 5,000 loans per month target
- Launch mobile app

**Q4 2026: National Rollout**
- All 50 states licensed and operational
- 15,000 loans per month target
- Launch auto refinance product

**Year 2 Goals:**
- 68,000 loans funded annually
- 15% market share in millennial professional segment
- 4.8-star app rating
- <3% monthly customer churn rate
