# AI-Powered Customer Service Platform

## Market Opportunity

Global customer service AI market projected to reach $58.4 billion by 2028 (CAGR 24.3% from $15.7B in 2024). Key drivers: labor cost inflation (average call center agent cost $42,000/year US, 18% increase since 2021) and customer expectation for 24/7 availability.

## Competitive Landscape

**Leading Platforms:**
- **Zendesk AI**: 287,000 business customers, processes 2.3 billion customer interactions annually
- **Intercom**: Resolution Bot resolves 33% of customer inquiries without human intervention
- **Ada**: Used by 1,200+ brands including Meta, Shopify, and Verizon
- **Salesforce Einstein**: 88% customer satisfaction for AI-resolved cases (vs. 82% for human-only)

**Market Gap:** Existing solutions struggle with complex multi-turn conversations requiring domain expertise. Our ML models trained on 47 million historical support tickets achieve 67% first-contact resolution vs. industry average 51%.

### Continuous Learning
- Model retraining weekly using human agent feedback
- Improvement rate: 2.3% accuracy gain per month
- Custom domain fine-tuning for specialized industries (legal, medical, financial)
