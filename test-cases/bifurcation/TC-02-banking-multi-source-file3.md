# Digital Lending Platform - Risk and Compliance

## Regulatory Compliance Requirements

### Federal Regulations
- **Truth in Lending Act (TILA)**: Clear disclosure of APR, fees, payment terms
- **Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA)**: Fair lending practices, no discrimination
- **Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA)**: Proper use and disclosure of credit reports
- **Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA)**: Privacy and data security requirements
- **Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) / Anti-Money Laundering (AML)**: Customer identification, suspicious activity reporting

### State Lending Licenses
Must obtain lending licenses in all 50 states. Average timeline: 6-12 months per state. Cost: $25,000-$100,000 per state for initial licensing and ongoing compliance.

## Credit Risk Management

### Default Rate Benchmarks
Industry average default rates by credit tier:
- Prime (FICO 720+): 1.8% annual default rate
- Near-prime (FICO 660-719): 4.2% annual default rate
- Subprime (FICO <660): 12.7% annual default rate

### Portfolio Composition Targets
- 40% prime borrowers (lower risk, lower margin)
- 35% near-prime borrowers (moderate risk, moderate margin)
- 25% subprime borrowers (higher risk, higher margin)

Target blended portfolio yield: 14.2% annual percentage yield (APY)
Target blended default rate: <5.5%
Target net interest margin: >8.0%

## Operational Risks

### Technology Failures
- Single point of failure risk: Mitigated through multi-region cloud deployment
- Data breach risk: $4.24 million average cost per breach (2024 IBM Security Report)
- API dependency risk: 99.9% uptime SLA required from all critical vendors

### Fraud Risk
Digital lending fraud losses averaged $142 per funded loan in 2024 (up from $89 in 2022). Synthetic identity fraud represents 67% of digital lending fraud cases. Investment in fraud detection tools: $2-3 million annually for mid-size lender.

## Funding and Liquidity Risk

Funding sources:
- Warehouse credit facilities: $500 million revolving credit line at LIBOR + 2.75%
- Securitization: Quarterly asset-backed securities (ABS) issuance
- Whole loan sales: To institutional investors at 102-105% of principal

Liquidity requirement: Maintain 60 days of operational expenses in cash reserves.
