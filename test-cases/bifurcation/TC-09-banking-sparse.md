# Credit Card Rewards Program Update

## Current Program

Our Platinum Rewards credit card offers 1% cash back on all purchases. Customer acquisition has slowed. Churn rate is 18% annually.

## Proposed Changes

Increase cash back to 2% on groceries and gas, 3% on dining, 1% on everything else.

## Competition

Chase Sapphire: 3% dining, 2% travel
Citi Double Cash: 2% all purchases
Capital One Quicksilver: 1.5% all purchases

## Investment

Estimated incremental cost: $4.7 million annually in increased rewards
Marketing campaign: $1.2 million
