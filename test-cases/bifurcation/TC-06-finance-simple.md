# ESG Investment Fund Launch

## Fund Overview

Launch sustainable investment fund focused on companies with strong Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) ratings.

## Market Opportunity

Global ESG assets under management: $35 trillion in 2024, projected $53 trillion by 2027 (BlackRock Global Investor Survey). Millennial and Gen Z investors allocate 89% of portfolios to ESG-aligned investments vs. 42% for Baby Boomers.

## Fund Strategy

**Investment Criteria:**
- MSCI ESG Rating of A or higher
- Carbon emissions 40% below industry average
- Board diversity: minimum 30% women and underrepresented groups
- No tobacco, weapons, fossil fuels, or private prisons

**Target Portfolio:**
- 60% large-cap US equities (S&P 500 ESG leaders)
- 25% international developed markets (MSCI EAFE ESG)
- 10% green bonds and sustainable fixed income
- 5% cash and alternatives

**Performance Benchmarks:**
- Primary: MSCI USA ESG Select Index
- Secondary: S&P 500 (for comparison to traditional strategies)
- Target: Match or exceed MSCI USA ESG Select by 0.5% annually net of fees

## Competitive Analysis

Major ESG funds and AUM:
- Vanguard ESG U.S. Stock ETF (ESGV): $8.2 billion AUM, 0.09% expense ratio
- iShares MSCI USA ESG Select ETF (SUSA): $5.7 billion AUM, 0.25% expense ratio
- Parnassus Core Equity Fund (PRBLX): $18.4 billion AUM, 0.82% expense ratio, actively managed

**Our Differentiation:**
- Lower expense ratio: 0.65% (competitive for actively managed)
- Quarterly ESG impact reporting (carbon avoided, diversity metrics)
- Shareholder advocacy: Engage companies on ESG improvement
